About Women In DeFi

About

Women in Defi is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting knowledge and empowerment among women in the areas of Blockchain Technology and Decentralized Finance. Our fundamental approach is to provide our community members with high-quality mentorship that focuses on their individual areas of expertise within the Blockchain Technology domain.

Our Mission

We envision a thriving, inclusive community of women in the Blockchain space, where diverse talents and perspective converge to revolutionize the Web3 landscape. Through mentorship, education, and collaboration, we strive to break down barriers, fostering innovation and leading the way towards a more equitable digital future.

Our Vision

We aim to educate 2,000,000 girls and women about blockchain technology, fostering understanding, creating opportunities, and nurturing future leaders in the blockchain space.

Inspired by a Movement: Our Roots in Women in DeFi

Women In DeFi was born from the powerful vision and foundational work laid by Sarah Idahosa,Founder. We are deeply inspired by their unwavering commitment to financial literacy, empowerment, and closing the gender gap in the decentralized finance space.
